ABSTRACT

This project proposes an Efficient Rain Fall Irrigation System (ERFIS) for crop based Precise Irrigation (PI), which helps to mitigate water scarcity. This ERFIS makes autonomous decisions about when and how much to irrigate, assisting farmers in accurately irrigating their farms based on the water requirements of specific crops. WHAT DO WE FOCUS? Currently, agriculture accounts (on average) for 70 percent of all freshwater withdrawals globally. In 2022,water scarcity affects the world by 30 to 40 percent of the world ,a report is given by Aarhus university. By 2040, water scarcity will affect the world by 50 to 60 percent of the world. So, we decided to develop an smart irrigation system which could save water efficiently. UNIQUENESS When comparing with the other forms of irrigation such as Surface irrigation, Drip irrigation, Sprinkler irrigation, the Rainfall irrigation is more sustainable and more beneficial both economically and ecologically. As the irrigation is set up in a Rain-like fashion from the top, the irrigation is done efficiently as the water reaches evenly to all the surface of the land.
